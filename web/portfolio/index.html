A product builder with a creative communication background.

I've spent years doing creative communication for IT. Somewhere in there, I realised I want to make products that not only sound good but also have an idea behind them. My strong belief is that when everyone can build anything with just a prompt, ideas and new angles are becoming priceless. Any product has its voice, whether it was designed or not — so I'd rather pick it on purpose.
