# What to Test — Nowhere

## 1. Onboarding
Go through the full onboarding. Grant HealthKit and Screen Time permissions. Sign in with Apple. Dismiss the paywall at the end.

## 2. Canvas
Pick options for Body, Mind, Heart — shapes should appear. Drag finger to smudge. Try different palettes, textures, and animation styles in Settings → Appearance.

## 3. Blocking apps
Create a group, add an app. Open that app — a shield should block it. Tap the shield button, pay with your balance to unlock.

## 4. Subscriptions
Open the paywall, check prices look correct. Try purchasing any plan (sandbox, no real charge). Locked features should unlock. Try "Restore Purchases".

## 5. Widgets
Add a Nowhere widget to your home screen. Check it shows your current data.

## 6. Account
Edit Profile → check nickname/avatar saving. Verify "Delete Account" button exists at the bottom (don't tap it).

## 7. Stability
Try: airplane mode, dark mode, largest text size, kill & relaunch. Nothing should crash, data should persist.
