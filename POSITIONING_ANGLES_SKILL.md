# Positioning angles — skill playbook (Nowhere)

**Purpose:** Reusable guide for *how* to position **Nowhere** in market and copy.

**Sources:** `MARKETING_COMPETITOR_RESEARCH.md` (especially §1 competitor map, §7 campaign implications, §10 market gaps, §8 URLs before paid spend) and **`PROJECT_STRATEGY.md`** for product facts, vocabulary, and core loop.

**Use this file for:** App Store copy, ads, landing pages, pitch decks, listicle outreach, internal creative briefs — whenever you need a **non-default** frame (not “another screen-time / focus app”).

**Related:** `TONE_OF_VOICE.md` (voice rules), `BRANDBOOK.md` (visual/brand system).

---

## 1. North star (do not drift)

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Canonical pitch** | *“Your life makes colors. Your feeds cost them.”* |
| **Lead frame** | Daily **canvas** → life produces **colors** → **spend** to open **feeds** (blocked app groups). |
| **Product loop (strategy)** | **Live** (steps, sleep, **pieces** in body / mind / heart) → **See** (canvas fills) → **Spend** (PayGate; canvas drains visibly). |
| **Do not lead with** | “Screen-time blocker,” “#1 focus app,” “cut time in half,” addiction-war / fight-the-phone framing — unless you are **deliberately** A/B testing against research. |

**Whitespace to own in ads and landing:** *aesthetic + philosophy of the day* (canvas, body / mind / heart, “nowhere / now here”) while the category competes on rankings, minutes saved, and “block better.”

### Default stack (most assets)

| Role | Angle | Use |
|------|-------|-----|
| **Primary** | **A** | Hero headline and first screenshot — *day as something you see*. |
| **Proof** | **C** | Subhead, demo, video — *visible depletion* when you open feeds (same story as A, not a competing frame). |
| **Comparison copy** | **B** | When reader thinks “steps for Instagram” or “focus score” — *one economy, three dimensions + sleep*. |
| **Tone** | **D** | How you write — especially community and long copy; not usually the hero line alone. |
| **Widget / store secondary** | **E** | Avoid confusion with launcher widgets; correct **job to be done**. |
| **Distribution** | **F** | Listicle and roundup *placement* — not a substitute for A as brand position. |

## 2. Positioning angles (lenses — usually **one primary + one proof** per asset)

Each angle is a **wedge** competitors under-serve. Match **channel** and **audience**; do not cram all six into one hero.

### Angle A — Life as artifact (not “screen time”)

- **Claim:** Your **day is something you see** before “we block bad apps.”
- **Vs market:** Most apps lead with control, time saved, productivity, fight-back framing.
- **Hooks:** “Day as canvas,” “colors from your life,” “opening a feed drains the picture.”
- **Proof:** **Visual** — generative canvas, optional Metal smudge, wallpaper / gradient — **not** a dashboard of percentages.

### Angle B — Holistic economy (not steps→scroll, not scores)

- **Claim:** One daily economy from **steps + sleep + intentional pieces** across **body / mind / heart**. User-facing word for daily choices is **pieces** (`PROJECT_STRATEGY.md` vocabulary); colors are **not** something you buy to skip that philosophy (align paywall with Notes / “can’t buy colors” stance).
- **Vs Fitlock / Steptime:** They read as “move more, scroll less.” You read as **systems + canvas + three-part life model.**
- **Vs Opal / gamified cluster:** They own scoreboards, streaks, pets, XP. You keep **anti-trophy** voice (`TONE_OF_VOICE.md`) with a **stronger visual story** than typical “pure focus” apps.

### Angle C — Visible trade (not only interrupt, not only hard block)

- **Claim:** You **see** balance and **see** the canvas **deplete** when you choose feeds — **emotional visibility of the trade.**
- **Relationship to A:** C is the **mechanism** that makes A believable in-product; pair them on cold traffic.
- **Vs one sec:** Not only a *pause* at open; a **daily visual economy.**
- **Vs Jomo / Freedom / Opal / Brick:** They stress rules, strict modes, or hardware ritual **as separate stories**; you bundle **enforcement + embodied feedback** in one metaphor (shields + draining canvas).

### Angle D — Voice as moat (literary / dry / observational)

- **Claim:** **Essay-grade, non-hype** — observational, not corporate metrics or clinical pamphlet copy.
- **Vs one sec:** Less “peer-reviewed habit fix,” more **philosophy of the day.**
- **Vs Opal:** Less leaderboard / community score energy, more **private, literary** register.
- **Channels:** r/digitalminimalism, r/nosurf, r/QuantifiedSelf, design Twitter, Are.na — audiences tired of **wellness SaaS stats.**

### Angle E — Widget semantics (state + spend, not launcher)

- **Claim:** Home Screen space shows **state of a personal economy** (energy, app groups, unlock path) — **not** a shortcut grid or social photo widget.
- **Vs Smile / Blank Spaces:** They optimize **faster path to allowed apps** and a calm grid. **Do not compete on that axis.** Widget and short descriptions should read **balance / feeds / spend** so Nowhere is not mistaken for a **dumbphone-style launcher.**

### Angle F — Category listicles (“third slot”)

- **What it is:** **Outreach and placement** — not a core brand claim.
- **Claim to editors:** Roundups default to Opal / Freedom / Forest — **own the “canvas / intentional life” row** with **one sentence** of differentiation plus **distinctive visual** creative.

## 3. Contrast map (consumer copy: reframe without naming brands)

| If they think we’re like… | Reframe to… |
|---------------------------|-------------|
| **Pause / breath before open** | Daily **visual economy**, not only an interruption. |
| **Steps for Instagram** | **Body / mind / heart + sleep + canvas**, not a single fitness meter. |
| **Focus score / community / leaderboard** | **Private canvas**, observational voice — not another scoreboard. |
| **Pets, streaks, XP** | **Anti-trophy** tone + **generative canvas** — not gamified stakes. |
| **Minimal home screen / launcher** | **Enforcement + economy**; launchers ease access to chosen apps; you **price opens** of feeds using life inputs. |

---

## 4. Guardrails

**Avoid these lanes without proprietary data:**

- “#1 screen time app,” headline **% time saved** arms races.
- **Science / reduction %** claims — costly to defend against evidence-first competitors.

**Product ↔ paywall:**

- If subscriptions cover tickets / insights / widgets, keep messaging consistent with **colors earned from life, not bought to bypass the loop** — so the paywall does not read as **pay-to-void the premise.**

**Optional “stacks” narrative (B2B, power users):**

- Minimal launcher + Nowhere can **coexist**: launcher = **quick allowed opens**; Nowhere = **shields + economy.**

## 5. Quick brief template (copy-paste)

1. **Primary angle:** A–F (default: **A** + proof **C**)
2. **One-line proof:** canvas visual / visible drain / three categories / widget job / tone line
3. **Contrast (internal):** which competitor-shaped expectation are we defusing?
4. **Forbidden for this asset:** e.g. #1, cut in half, generic “block distractions” as hero
5. **CTA tone:** observational, not hype (`TONE_OF_VOICE.md`)
